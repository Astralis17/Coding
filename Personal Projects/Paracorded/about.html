Who are we?

Paracorded is a TY Mini-Company selling a number of paracord based products.
Working with paracord is something we enjoy a great deal, and we want to share our love for the craft with you.


What is parachute cord?

Parachute cord also known as paracord for short, is the cord that is used in parachutes by nearly all mainstream operatives. But paracord isn't just for mainstream operatives, we're here to spread it to as many people as possible, with our paracord supplier being eco-friendly and reliably serving customers for over 10 years.

Parachute cord is a recyclable and eco-friendly cord that can support 550lbs/250kg with no sign of damage.
